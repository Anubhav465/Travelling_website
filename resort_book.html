About Soneva Fushi

Soneva Fushi is an award-winning luxury resort located in the breathtaking Baa Atoll in the Maldives. This eco-friendly resort offers beachfront villas with private pools and unparalleled ocean views. Guests can enjoy a range of activities, including snorkeling, diving, and stargazing at the resort's observatory.

Special Amenities:

  • Private beachfront villas with pools
  • World-class dining and wine cellar
  • Overwater spa with Ayurvedic treatments
  • Swimming pools with ocean views
  • Exclusive cinema on the beach
